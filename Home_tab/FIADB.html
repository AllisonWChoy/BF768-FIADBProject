Forest Data Inventory

Summary abour our database

This database is for Massachusetts forests using the national forest inventory database provided by the US Forest Service. The user will be able to grab information about Massachusetts forests trees. There are seven main tables used as a part of our database. Tree, damage, species, location, mortality, growth, and biomass tables. Those tables will be used for our main queries to allow the user to detect change over time. For example: change in mean steam density, growth, and mortality rate at the tree level.

The national forest inventory data is provided by the US Forest Service, containing information to assess Americaâ€™s forests. There are over 50 years of data, 20 of which is relailable under a single design. The data contains information about tree characteristics collected from trail plots where surveyors noted the latitude and longitude, diameter, health status, vegetation in the crown, number tree rings, biomass, volume, and plot level information. A limitation of the data includes scrambled longitude and latitude on each tree plot in order to conserve and protect the locations of certain forests.
